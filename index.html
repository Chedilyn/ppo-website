Our Core Services

Population Data and Information Services

Provides population statistics, research, and demographic information to support planning and decision-making in communities.

Family Planning Services

Provides information and counseling on responsible parenthood and family planning methods to help families make informed reproductive health decisions.

Adolescent Health and Development Program

Educates young people about reproductive health, life skills, and responsible behavior to prevent early pregnancy and promote youth development.

VISION

The Provincial Population Office (PPO) is the lead office in the province in advancing the population and development policies and programs towards increased share and opportunity of each Filipino in socio-economic progress; for a healthy, resilient and empowered people, secure with a sustainable economy and ecologically balanced environment, anchored on accountable and transparent governance.

MISSION

To strengthen institutional capacities to responsibly serve the people of Misamis Oriental by implementing our mandates, collaborating with stakeholders to implement integrated population and development strategies, policies, and programs based on demographic and socio-economic data, information and knowledge, in support of the Honorable Governor Juliette T. Uy BANGON MIS. OR. Platform of Governance under:

B - Barangay Empowerment and Strengthening
N - Noble Health and Social Services Delivery
N - Notable Programs for Youth Development
